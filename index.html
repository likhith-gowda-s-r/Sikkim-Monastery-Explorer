Preserving Himalayan Spirituality

Sikkim is home to more than 77 Buddhist monasteries (gompas), each embodying centuries of Tibetan Buddhist architecture, art, and devotion. This digital showcase aims to preserve and promote these sacred sites for pilgrims, tourists, and future generations.
